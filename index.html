Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Flo ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Data you enter voluntarily: income amounts, spending categories, and financial figures you type into the app.
  • Usage data: page views, tool usage, and general analytics (no personal identifiers).
  • No bank data: We never connect to your bank account, credit card, or any financial institution.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • All calculations happen in your browser. Financial figures are stored locally in your device (localStorage) and never sent to our servers.
  • Anonymous usage analytics help us improve the product.

3. Data Storage

Your budget data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Clearing your browser data will erase it. We do not have access to this data.

4. Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies only (language preference, session state). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use anonymous analytics (e.g. Plausible Analytics, which is GDPR-compliant and cookie-free) and Chart.js for data visualization loaded from a CDN.

6. Your Rights

  • You can clear all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • GDPR / CCPA: You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Contact us at privacy@getflo.io.

7. Children

Flo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We'll note the date of the latest revision at the top of this page.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@getflo.io
